Common questions

What are research peptides?
Research peptides are synthetic amino acid chains used exclusively in laboratory settings. They are not intended for human or animal use and must be handled by qualified research professionals.
How is each batch tested?
Every True Life Peptides batch undergoes independent HPLC and mass spectrometry testing by certified third-party laboratories. COA documents are available on request for any batch.

Orders placed before 2PM EST ship the same business day. All shipments are discreet, insured, and temperature-protected where required. We ship within the continental United States.
