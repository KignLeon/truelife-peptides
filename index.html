Frequently Asked Questions

What are research peptides?
Research peptides are short chains of amino acids synthesized for laboratory research. They are not for human or animal consumption and are used exclusively in controlled scientific environments.
How are your products tested?
Every batch is independently tested via HPLC and mass spectrometry by third-party laboratories. Certificates of Analysis (COA) are available for every product batch we produce.
