GMP-Compliant Manufacturing

Every peptide is synthesized in GMP-compliant facilities within the United States using advanced solid-phase peptide synthesis (SPPS). We never outsource production overseas.

Third-Party Batch Testing

Every production batch undergoes independent HPLC and mass spectrometry analysis. Certificates of Analysis (COA) are available for every batch we produce and ship.

Research-Grade Purity

Our peptides consistently achieve ≥98% purity through rigorous quality control inspections, ensuring researchers receive compounds that meet the highest standards for accuracy.

Same-Day Shipping

Orders placed before 2PM EST ship the same business day. All shipments are discreet, insured, and temperature-protected to preserve compound integrity during transit.

Certificate of Analysis (COA)

Every product we sell comes with a verifiable Certificate of Analysis. Each COA includes HPLC chromatograms, mass spectrometry data, and amino acid analysis results. Our commitment to transparency means you can verify the purity and identity of every peptide before it enters your research.
