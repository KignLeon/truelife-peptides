Standards

What we stand for

GMP-Compliant Manufacturing

Every compound is synthesized in GMP-compliant US facilities using advanced solid-phase peptide synthesis (SPPS). We maintain documented quality management systems aligned with pharmaceutical manufacturing standards. We never outsource overseas.

Independent Third-Party Batch Testing

Every production batch undergoes independent HPLC purity analysis and mass spectrometry (MS) identity confirmation through accredited third-party laboratories. Certificates of Analysis (COA) are issued per lot and available on every order.

≥98% Purity — Non-Negotiable

Our compounds consistently achieve ≥98% purity through strict quality control during synthesis and rigorous post-production QC inspections. No batch is released for sale unless it meets or exceeds this threshold, regardless of timeline or demand.

Full Transparency — Always

We believe researchers deserve to know exactly what they're ordering. Our COA documentation includes the HPLC chromatogram, mass spectrum, amino acid composition analysis, lot number, and manufacturing date — fully traceable from synthesis to your lab.

Temperature-Controlled Shipping

All shipments are dispatched with appropriate temperature management to preserve compound integrity in transit. Orders placed before 2PM EST ship the same business day. Discreet, insured packaging on every order with full tracking.

Research Institutions & Wholesale

We supply individual researchers, licensed laboratories, and institutional purchasers. Volume pricing and wholesale accounts are available for research institutions. Contact us directly for institutional pricing, bulk orders, and custom formulation inquiries.
