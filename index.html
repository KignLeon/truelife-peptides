Frequently asked questions

What are research peptides used for?
Peptides are short chains of amino acids used in laboratory research to study biological processes, cellular signaling, and molecular interactions. All products sold by True Life Peptides are intended for in vitro laboratory research use only by qualified professionals.
Are your peptides independently tested?
Yes. Every production batch is independently tested by accredited third-party US laboratories using HPLC purity analysis and mass spectrometry identity confirmation. A Certificate of Analysis (COA) with the full chromatogram and lot number is available for every batch.
How fast is shipping?
Orders placed before 2PM EST ship the same business day. Standard delivery takes 2–3 business days within the continental United States. All shipments are temperature-controlled, discreet, and fully insured.
Where are your products manufactured?
All compounds are synthesized in GMP-compliant facilities in the United States using advanced solid-phase peptide synthesis (SPPS). We never outsource production overseas.
Do you offer wholesale or institutional pricing?
Yes. We work with research institutions, licensed laboratories, and qualified distributors. Contact us directly at +1 (972) 919-0219 or through our contact page for volume pricing and wholesale account setup.
